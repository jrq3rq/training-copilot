# Capstone Project Guide

**Project Title:** Build Your Mini Self-Reliance Station

**Final Project for Builder Program – Year 1**

This is your chance to show everything you’ve learned. You will design, plan, build, and present a small functional “Self-Reliance Station” that demonstrates multiple skills from the year.

### Project Goal

Create a practical, real-world station that helps your family become more self-reliant. It should combine at least 4–6 different skills you learned during the program.

### Project Requirements

Your station must include:

- A clear purpose (what problem does it solve?)
- Planning and budgeting
- At least 4 skills from the curriculum
- Safety considerations
- Documentation (photos, journal entries, reflection)
- A final presentation or demonstration

### Suggested Station Ideas

- Water Collection & Filtration Station
- Mini Food Production + Preservation Corner
- Emergency Preparedness Kit + Power Station
- Tool Repair & Maintenance Bench
- Indoor Herb Garden + Kitchen Helper Station
- Communication & Signaling Center
- Personal First Aid + Safety Station

You may also create your own idea.

### Step-by-Step Process

**Phase 1: Planning (Week 35)**

- Choose your station idea
- List the skills it will demonstrate
- Draw a simple design / sketch
- Create a materials list and budget
- Set a realistic timeline

**Phase 2: Building (Week 35–36)**

- Gather materials
- Build the station safely
- Document your process with photos

**Phase 3: Presentation & Reflection (End of Week 36)**

- Present your station to family or group
- Explain what skills you used
- Share what worked well and what was challenging
- Complete your final Builder Journal reflection

### Difficulty Tiers

- **Explorer Tier**: Build a simple version with heavy adult help
- **Builder Tier**: Build independently with some guidance
- **Pathfinder Tier**: Design, plan, and lead the entire project

### Neurodivergent Supports

- Break the project into small daily tasks
- Use visual checklists
- Include regulation breaks
- Allow choice in how you document (photos, drawings, video, writing)
- Work with a partner or family member if preferred

### Final Deliverables

1. Completed Mini Self-Reliance Station
2. Planning documents (design, budget, timeline)
3. Photo documentation of the build
4. Builder Journal reflection
5. Short presentation (can be to family)

You’ve worked hard all year — this is your moment to shine and show how far you’ve come.

You’ve got this, Builder!
